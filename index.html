About BioSky

Building the intelligence layer

How It Works

From space to decision.
Four seamless steps.

1

Observe

Multi-sensor satellites capture optical and radar Earth observation data — solar irradiance, cloud cover, atmospheric conditions, and surface temperatures across target regions.

2

Integrate

Satellite imagery is fused with weather API feeds and grid telemetry data, cleaned, normalized, and structured for AI model consumption.

3

Predict

Proprietary AI/ML models generate sub-hourly energy forecasts, detect grid anomalies, and model climate risk scenarios with high accuracy.

4

Deliver

Actionable insights reach operators through BioSky's decision intelligence platform — real-time dashboards, APIs, and automated alerts for energy decision support.
